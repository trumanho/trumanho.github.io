My Story

In 2010,I began my studies in applied biology at the City University of Hong Kong. I had joined the Executive Committee of the Society as financial secretary, and had organized various events throughout the year.In 2011,I started studies with minor environmental science. After completed my term of secretary, I played a role in Council of Students’Union as event financial councilor, reviewing the work of the new Executive Committee.

In 2012,I had joined the internship of Education Department of Kadoorie Farm & Botanic Garden (KFBG) during the summer, providing guided eco-tour and organizing educational exhibitions in Macau. At the end of the year, I chose to atart my academic research on the emotional effect of chronic pain via animal behavioral tests. Unfortunately, the experimental results were not satisfactory, and I did not participated in further study on the topic.

In 2013,I graduated with upper second class honors. During the college exhibition, my research report has been chosen as a representative of the department and presented to new students.

In 2013,I had my first job in a new sportswear retail shop. I was responsible for every tiny part of the store and solved different problems in the startup period. I had also organized various events during the time. After the shop has become steady, I left the shop.

In 2014,I was employed by a trading company, to assist in distribution of viral diagnostic reagents. My primary works were to provide product demonstration and document preparation.

Between 2014-2015,I participated in different medical exhibitions on behalf of the company.At the end of 2014,I had changed my working hour due to some family issues. I started working for a travel agency, focusing on preparation of web-based information and some design work at home.

At the end of 2016,I left the company after working for nearly three years. Looking forward to find a better job, to enable me to contribute more.
